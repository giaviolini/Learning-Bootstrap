Welcome to the latest updates and events in the world of Animal Crossing: New Horizons!

As the summer days turn to autumn, the world of Animal Crossing is abuzz with exciting events, seasonal changes, and new surprises for all islanders. Get ready to immerse yourself in a plethora of activities that will keep you engaged and entertained throughout August and September. From limited-time events to special visitors and thematic celebrations, let's explore what's in store for you on your beloved island!

Summer Splash Event (August 1st - August 31st)

Dive into the Summer Splash event and celebrate the sunny season with a splash! Join the fun as your island transforms into a beachside paradise. Take part in water-based activities, like swimming, diving, and exploring the ocean floor for precious sea creatures and mermaid-themed DIY recipes. Collect shells and encounter new characters while earning exclusive summertime rewards. Don't forget to check out the stylish summer outfits at Able Sisters' shop to keep you cool during the hot days.

Bonfire Night (August 28th)

On the last Saturday of August, gather your animal villagers and friends for a memorable Bonfire Night celebration. Roast marshmallows, sing campfire songs, and enjoy the mesmerizing sight of fireworks illuminating the night sky. Don't miss the chance to capture stunning pictures with your favorite villagers against this enchanting backdrop. It's a night of warmth, camaraderie, and making memories that will last a lifetime.

Autumn Harvest Festival (September 21st - September 30th)

As summer comes to an end, prepare to welcome the vibrant colors of fall with the Autumn Harvest Festival. Venture into the woods and gather seasonal ingredients for delectable autumn-themed recipes. Set up a Thanksgiving feast at your home and invite your villagers to share in the delicious treats. Help Franklin the turkey in his cooking quest, and you'll receive unique rewards and a warm sense of community.

Time Traveler's Delight (August 1st - September 30th)

For time travelers, August and September offer a unique opportunity to visit previous seasonal events from the past. Revisit beloved festivals, like Fireworks Show and Bug-Off, and rediscover rare DIY recipes and event-exclusive items. It's a chance to relive cherished memories or experience events you might have missed.

New Items in Nook's Cranny and Able Sisters

Keep an eye out for fresh and exciting items available at Nook's Cranny and Able Sisters' shop. Embrace the latest trends with stylish clothing and accessories. Plus, expand your home decor with beautiful autumn-themed furniture to capture the essence of the season.

Stay Tuned for Updates!

Remember, this is just a glimpse of the featured content coming to Animal Crossing in August and September 2023. The game's developers at Nintendo are continually working to surprise and delight you with new experiences and seasonal events. Keep an eye on your NookPhone for announcements and updates, and be ready for the endless wonders that await you in this magical world of Animal Crossing! Happy island living!
